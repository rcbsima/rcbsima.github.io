The lease has a large and visible facade and three wide windows facing Købmagergade.

The lease is comprised of 336 sqm. on the ground floor and has a regular shape. The lease is visible and located in the beginning of Købmagergade, when comming from Nørreport Station.

Købmagegade stretches from Kultorvet to Amagertorv and Strøget and is the main connection between Strøget and Nørreport.
