Intro tekst

Beskrivelse

French outerwear experts, Moncler, used to occupy the premises of Østergade 16 untill recently, when Moncler moved futher down the street of Østergade to find more space.

Moncler left behind a beautiful store in a well-established area, where customers find Hugo Boss, Max Mara, Mulberry and Zadig & Voltaire.
The premises are bright and inviting, and the high ceiling ensures a rather good indoor environment.

Approximately half way in the premises, the store has large window sections facing the courtyard ensuring optimal inflow of light during all hours of the day.
