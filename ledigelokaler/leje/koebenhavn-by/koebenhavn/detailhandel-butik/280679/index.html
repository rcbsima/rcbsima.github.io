Beskrivelse

The lease is a part of Industriens Hus, a building erected in 2013 that has a large, iconic facade with a changing light display that demands attention. The lease is located at Vesterbrogade and has big windows facing Axel Towers.

Industriens Hus houses among others McDonald’s, Irma, Crème by Nimb and offices from 1st floor and above.

The lease comprises 296 m2 sales area on the ground floor, a share of common area of 26 m2, 24 m2 technical facilities and 40 m2 store room on the first floor, which totals up to 386 m2.
