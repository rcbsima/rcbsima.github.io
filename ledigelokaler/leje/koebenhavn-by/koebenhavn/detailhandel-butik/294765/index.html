Title: Købmagergade 19 - 1150 København K

Intro tekst

ABSOLUTELY PRIME LOCATION

Flashline

IMPRESSIVE FRONTAGE AND VISIBILITY

Beskrivelse

The lease offers an impressive double heighted frontage and exposure, with optimal display and a wide glass entrance - totalling app. 7 metres.

Both the frontage and the entire lease is currently under re-construction for an optimal layout.

Inside one will find a quite flexible sales area, which can be used for various product displays and interior settings combined with spacious double ceiling height in the front of the sales area towards Købmagergade and app. 2,9 metres in the middle section and app. 3,25 in the back area of the lease.

The ground floor level is app. 603 m² in total and provides direct access via both an internal staircase and an elevator to the well-preserved storage area in the basement of app. 371 m².

As an alternative, a section of the ground floor area in the back of the lease can be used for storage.

The lease will be handed over according to ”White Box” specification and subject to further agreement with the landlord.

Attractive flagship store opportunity for the distinguished retailer !

- you will not be disappointed.
