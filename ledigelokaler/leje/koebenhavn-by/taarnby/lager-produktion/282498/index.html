Title: Kystvejen 28 - 2770 Kastrup

Intro tekst

4,757 sq m warehouse with up to 1,800 sq m office

Flashline

Logistics project at Kastrup airside

Beskrivelse

DEVELOPMENT IN THE AREACopenhagen Airport is undergoing rapid development. With 175 routes, and more than 700 daily operations, it is one of the fastest growing in Europe. The airport´s goal is to be able to fulfil the growth plan for an annual increase in capacity from 29 to 40 million passengers.The expansion will increase the need for new logistics facilities, and to this end, AEW has acquired a property that gives the tenant a unique opportunity to be part of this growth potential. With an airside location, and short distances to infrastructure, this development gives you the opportunity to move into one of Scandinavia’s most attractive logistics hubs.FREIGHT VOLUMEFreight volumes have stabilized after reaching the airport’s maximum capacity in 2014. The airport has continued to run at maximum capacity since then. With the expansion of airside buildings and growth in routes and passengers, freight volumes are expected to increase as capacity grows.
