Beskrivelse

Viborg is located in central Jutland approx. 45 km from the city of Randers and approx. 65 km from the city of Aarhus. Viborg has a large selection of both private and public workspaces. The city is attractive to both young people, families and elderly, as Viborg houses large sports and cultural events as well as day care centres, schools and other higher education facilities.

There is a general focus on improving infrastructure internally as well as to and from Viborg Municipality. Today there is several main roads to large cities in Jutland, such as Route 26 to Aarhus, Route 16 to Randers and Route 52 to Silkeborg.

The Property is easily accessible in terms of both public and private transportation. The closest bus stop is located just 200 metres from the Property, and Viborg Station (train and bus) is just 550 metres away. Furthermore, the Property is located next to ‘Indre Ringvej’ and ‘Søndre Ringvej’ making the Property easily accessible by car.
